Ashmi Feyhr

Slight for a viera, Ashmi is an expressive woman with large, animated grey-green eyes and a friendly disposition. Her fair complexion outs her as one of the Veena, although the brocade dresses and dyed tunics she enjoys wearing have come from far beyond the distant wood.

Her tall leoporine ears are woolly and as dark as her hair. They often precede her in a crowd.

She enjoys the following foods:

  • Cabbage
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Beans
